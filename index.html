About LUWI Foundation

LUWI Foundation is a student-centered organization committed to creating safe, empowering, and inclusive learning environments for pupils and students in Zambia.

We support learners facing academic pressure, mental health challenges, financial hardship, gender-based violence (GBV), menstrual hygiene challenges, disability barriers, early marriages, unwanted pregnancies, and exploitation.

Our Vision & Mission

  • Establish Safe Student Support Hubs
  • Launch LUWI Scholarship & Emergency Fund
  • Partner with Schools & Universities
  • Create Talent & Innovation Incubation Programs
  • Build a Model School & University
  • Achieve Financial Sustainability

Mission: Support, empower, and nurture students through mentorship, academic assistance, awareness programs, and safe spaces.

Vision: A safe, empowering, and affordable educational ecosystem in Zambia where every student thrives.

Our Programs & Activities
