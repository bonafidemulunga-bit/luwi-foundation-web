Welcome to LUWI Foundation

LUWI Foundation is committed to empowering students and communities in Zambia through education, healthcare, emergency relief, and social empowerment. Our programs reach thousands of learners and families every year, providing hope, support, and opportunity.

We believe that every student deserves a safe environment to grow academically, socially, and creatively. Join us in our mission to transform lives.
