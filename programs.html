Our Programs

1. Education Support Program

We believe education is the key to breaking the cycle of poverty. Our program provides:

  • School fees and uniforms for vulnerable children
  • Books, stationery, and learning materials
  • Mentorship and guidance to help students achieve their dreams

2. Community Empowerment

Empowering individuals to transform their communities through:

  • Skills training for youth and women
  • Entrepreneurship and livelihood programs
  • Workshops that strengthen leadership and confidence

3. Emergency Relief

Providing timely support to families in crisis by offering:

  • Food and essential supplies
  • Basic healthcare assistance
  • Temporary shelter and emergency support services

4. Awareness Programs

We run educational campaigns, health talks, and community workshops to raise awareness on critical social and health issues. Our activities include:

  • Menstrual hygiene management and reproductive health
  • Mental health awareness and counseling
  • Preventing early marriages, unwanted pregnancies, and intersex challenges
  • Gender-Based Violence (GBV) prevention and education
  • Risks associated with sugar daddies and sugar mommas
  • Support and inclusion for persons with disabilities
  • Sexual and reproductive health services (SRH) awareness
  • School and university visits to give health talks and interact with students and pupils
  • Orphanage visits to provide guidance, mentorship, and care
  • Community outreach programs and community service projects

Through these programs, LUWI Foundation Zambia engages directly with schools, universities, orphanages, and communities to educate, empower, and create safe, informed, and healthy environments for all.
