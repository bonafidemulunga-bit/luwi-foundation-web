About LUWI Foundation

LUWI Foundation is a non-profit organization committed to empowering students and vulnerable communities in Zambia through education, health, emergency relief, and social programs. Our goal is to create lasting impact by supporting learners and families in need.

Our Story

Founded by Bonafide Mulunga, LUWI Foundation was born from personal struggle and determination. After excelling in Grade 12, Bonafide received a full government scholarship but was placed in a program that did not match his passion. Facing financial hardship and emotional challenges, he persevered and later pursued a Bachelor of Science in Nursing.

Inspired by his journey, Bonafide envisioned a foundation that ensures no student feels unsupported, unheard, or forced to bury their talents. “LUWI” means “God’s Mercy,” symbolizing hope, light, and compassion for every student.

Our Mission

To support, empower, and nurture students through mentorship, academic assistance, awareness programs, and safe spaces that enable them to thrive academically, socially, and emotionally.

Our Vision

To build a safe, inclusive, and empowering educational ecosystem in Zambia where all students have the opportunity to succeed, grow creatively, and contribute to their communities.

Our Values

  • Compassion and Empathy
  • Integrity and Accountability
  • Equity and Inclusion
  • Empowerment through Education
  • Community Engagement
