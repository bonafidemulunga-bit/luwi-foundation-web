About LUWI Foundation Zambia

Executive Summary

LUWI Foundation Zambia is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering students and communities across Zambia. We focus on education, healthcare, social empowerment, and emergency relief programs, reaching thousands of learners annually. Our mission is to create safe and supportive environments for students to thrive academically, socially, and creatively.

Since our inception, we have partnered with schools, universities, and local organizations to implement awareness programs, scholarships, counseling services, and talent development initiatives. Our efforts have impacted over 1,000 students so far, and we continue to expand our reach with every program and partnership.

Our Mission

LUWI Foundation Zambia is committed to empowering students and communities in Zambia through education, healthcare, emergency relief, and social empowerment. We believe every student deserves a safe and supportive environment to grow academically, socially, and creatively.

Our Vision

A Zambia where all students have access to education, guidance, and resources needed to unlock their potential and contribute positively to their communities.

Our Programs & Achievements

  • Student Mental Health & Counseling
  • Gender-Based Violence (GBV) Prevention
  • Educational Support & Scholarships
  • Emergency Relief & Crisis Response
  • Talent & Entrepreneurship Development
  • Women & Child Welfare Programs
  • Environmental Sustainability Initiatives

Through these initiatives, we have reached thousands of students and community members. Students have testified on the impact of our programs, and we continue to form partnerships with institutions such as the Zambian Royal Medical University and the Intersex Society of Zambia.

Get Involved

Join us in our mission to transform lives. Whether through donations, volunteering, or spreading awareness, every action makes a difference. Together, we can empower students and communities to achieve their full potential.
