About LUWI Foundation

LUWI Foundation Zambia is dedicated to empowering students and communities through education, healthcare, emergency relief, and social development programs.

Our mission is to ensure that every student has the resources and support they need to thrive academically, socially, and creatively. We are currently working with schools, students, and partners to create sustainable impact across Zambia.

We are proud to partner with organizations such as the Intersex Society of Zambia, Sports Impact Network, and the Zambian Royal Medical University to reach more than 1000 students and counting.

Our founder and team of dedicated lecturers, volunteers, and professionals ensure that LUWI Foundation programs are delivered efficiently, with measurable outcomes and community engagement at their core.
