About LUWI Foundation

Empowering Students | Protecting Futures | Transforming Communities

Our Mission

To provide educational opportunities, protect vulnerable youth, and promote holistic community development across Zambia.

Our Vision

To create empowered communities where every student has access to education, safety, and opportunities for growth.

Meet Our Founder

Bonafide Mulunga

Founder & CEO of LUWI Foundation Zambia. Bonafide is dedicated to transforming the lives of students and communities through education, mental health support, and protection programs. His leadership has inspired a new generation of youth advocates and change-makers in Zambia.
