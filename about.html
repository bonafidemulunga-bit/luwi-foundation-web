About LUWI Foundation

Executive Summary

LUWI Foundation is a Zambia-based non-profit organization committed to empowering students and young people across the country. Our mission goes beyond awareness: we foster leadership, mentorship, education, social development, and community engagement to prepare young people to thrive in school, university, and society.

Since our establishment, we have reached thousands of students, partnered with universities and organizations, delivered workshops, mentorship programs, and community initiatives, and provided platforms for young voices to be heard. LUWI Foundation is dedicated to creating lasting positive change by investing in youth, who are the backbone of Zambiaâ€™s future.

Mission

To equip students and young people in Zambia with knowledge, skills, and mentorship opportunities that empower them to achieve their potential academically, socially, and personally.

Vision

A Zambia where every student and young person has access to education, mentorship, health information, and leadership opportunities, fostering a generation of informed, confident, and socially responsible youth.

Core Values

  • Empowerment: We provide tools and opportunities that help young people succeed.
  • Inclusion: We value diversity and ensure every voice is heard and respected.
  • Integrity: We operate transparently and uphold the highest ethical standards.
  • Collaboration: We partner with schools, universities, communities, and organizations to maximize impact.
  • Impact: We focus on programs that deliver measurable and meaningful change.

Our Story

LUWI Foundation was born out of a passion for youth empowerment. Observing the challenges students faced in accessing mentorship, academic guidance, life skills, and social support, we realized that structured programs could transform lives. Our founder, a lecturer at Zambian Royal Medical University, combined academic experience with grassroots community engagement to create LUWI Foundation as a platform for student growth and leadership.

From small workshops in local schools to national outreach programs, LUWI Foundation has grown steadily, impacting thousands of students and creating partnerships that strengthen educational and community initiatives across Zambia.

Programs and Initiatives

Our programs are designed to address the multifaceted needs of students and young people:

  • Educational Workshops: Enhancing academic performance, study skills, critical thinking, and leadership.
  • Mentorship Programs: Connecting students with mentors in education, career development, and personal growth.
  • Leadership Development: Workshops, seminars, and initiatives that develop student leaders and advocates.
  • Health & Wellbeing Awareness: Covering physical, mental, and social health, including sexual health, nutrition, and stress management.
  • Community Engagement: Encouraging students to participate in community service, volunteerism, and social initiatives.
  • Advocacy and Awareness: Campaigns to raise awareness on important youth issues, reduce stigma, and promote inclusion.

Achievements

  • Reached over 1,000 students across multiple schools and universities.
  • Established partnerships with Intersex Society of Zambia, Sports Impact Network, and Zambian Royal Medical University.
  • Organized over 50 workshops and community events to promote youth development.
  • Created mentorship networks connecting students with professional and academic mentors.
  • Documented student testimonies to inform future programs and community advocacy.

Impact

LUWI Foundation has empowered students to achieve academic success, develop leadership skills, participate in community projects, and gain access to mentorship and guidance. Students report increased confidence, better decision-making, stronger social connections, and readiness to take active roles in society. Our programs have helped bridge the gap between academic learning and practical life skills, creating a generation of well-rounded, informed, and engaged youth.

Leadership

Our leadership team is composed of educators, mentors, and youth advocates who bring experience, passion, and expertise to our programs. The founder, a lecturer at Zambian Royal Medical University, ensures that all initiatives are research-informed, inclusive, and impactful. The team is committed to transparency, collaboration, and continuous growth to serve students effectively.

Community Engagement

LUWI Foundation encourages students to take active roles in their communities. Through service projects, awareness campaigns, and mentorship initiatives, we aim to instill a sense of responsibility, leadership, and civic engagement among youth. Our programs not only empower students individually but also strengthen communities collectively.
