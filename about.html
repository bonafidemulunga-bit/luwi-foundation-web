About LUWI Foundation

LUWI Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering vulnerable communities, promoting education, improving health, and providing relief in emergencies. Our mission is to create lasting change through innovative programs and compassionate service.

Our Mission

To provide sustainable support to underprivileged individuals and communities, focusing on education, health, and social empowerment, while inspiring hope and resilience.

Our Vision

To become a leading foundation in Africa, recognized for transforming lives and communities through impactful initiatives and partnerships that ensure equal opportunities for all.

Meet Our Founder

Bonafide Mulunga, the visionary behind LUWI Foundation, started this organization with a deep commitment to social justice and community development. Inspired by the challenges faced by vulnerable populations, Bonafide has dedicated his efforts to providing education, healthcare, and emergency support for those in need, creating a legacy of hope and empowerment.

About LUWI Foundation

LUWI Foundation is dedicated to transforming lives through education support, community empowerment, and sustainable development initiatives.

Our Mission

To provide educational support, scholarships, and essential resources to vulnerable children and youth in Zambia, empowering them to build brighter futures.

Our Vision

A Zambia where every child has equal access to quality education, opportunity, and the tools needed to succeed in life.

Founder’s Story

LUWI Foundation was founded with a passion to support vulnerable children who struggle to access education due to financial hardship. The foundation believes that education is the key to breaking the cycle of poverty and creating sustainable change in communities.
