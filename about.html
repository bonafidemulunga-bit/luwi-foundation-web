About LUWI Foundation

Executive Summary

LUWI Foundation is a Zambia-based non-profit organization dedicated to empowering students and young people across the country. Our mission is to foster holistic growth by nurturing academic excellence, leadership skills, talents, and social development while ensuring a balanced approach with extra-curricular activities.

Since our inception, we have reached thousands of students through workshops, mentorship programs, community initiatives, and partnerships with universities and organizations. LUWI Foundation is committed to creating lasting positive change by equipping young people with the tools, guidance, and opportunities to thrive in school, university, and life.

Mission

To equip students and young people in Zambia with knowledge, skills, mentorship, and opportunities that empower them to achieve their full potential academically, socially, personally, and creatively.

Vision

A Zambia where every student has access to education, mentorship, leadership opportunities, and platforms to develop their talents and skills, fostering a generation of informed, confident, and socially responsible youth.

Core Values

  • Empowerment: We provide tools, mentorship, and opportunities for young people to excel academically, socially, and creatively.
  • Inclusion: We value diversity and ensure every student’s voice and talent is recognized and respected.
  • Integrity: We operate transparently and uphold high ethical standards in all programs.
  • Collaboration: We partner with schools, universities, communities, and organizations to maximize impact.
  • Balance: We encourage students to balance academics, talent development, and extra-curricular activities.
  • Impact: We focus on programs that deliver measurable and meaningful outcomes.

Our Story

LUWI Foundation was born from a deep passion for youth empowerment. Observing the challenges students faced in academics, mentorship, and personal growth, we realized structured programs could transform lives. Our founder, a lecturer at Zambian Royal Medical University, combined academic expertise with grassroots community engagement to create LUWI Foundation as a platform for student success and leadership.

Starting with local school workshops and mentorship sessions, LUWI Foundation has grown to national outreach programs impacting thousands of students and forming strong partnerships that enhance educational and community initiatives across Zambia.

Programs and Initiatives

Our programs address the diverse needs of students and young people:

  • Educational Workshops: Enhancing academic performance, study skills, critical thinking, and leadership.
  • Mentorship Programs: Connecting students with experienced mentors for guidance in academics, career, and personal development.
  • Talent & Skills Nurturing: Helping students discover, develop, and showcase their talents in arts, sports, innovation, and entrepreneurship.
  • Leadership Development: Workshops, seminars, and initiatives to cultivate confident and responsible student leaders.
  • Health & Wellbeing Awareness: Covering physical, mental, and social health including nutrition, stress management, and personal care.
  • Extra-Curricular Balance: Programs that guide students to balance studies, talents, and extracurricular activities for well-rounded growth.
  • Community Engagement: Encouraging volunteerism and social responsibility through community service projects and campaigns.
  • Advocacy and Awareness: Campaigns to promote inclusion, reduce stigma, and raise awareness on important youth issues.

Achievements

  • Reached over 1,000 students across multiple schools, colleges, and universities.
  • Established partnerships with Intersex Society of Zambia, Sports Impact Network, and Zambian Royal Medical University.
  • Organized more than 50 workshops, talent showcases, and community events.
  • Created mentorship networks linking students with professional and academic mentors.
  • Documented student stories and testimonies to guide future programs and advocacy.

Impact

LUWI Foundation empowers students to achieve academic success, develop leadership skills, nurture talents, and actively engage in community projects. Our programs help students balance academics with creative and extracurricular pursuits, leading to well-rounded personal growth. Participants report increased confidence, better decision-making, improved social skills, and readiness to take on leadership roles in society.

Leadership

Our leadership team consists of educators, mentors, and youth advocates who bring experience, dedication, and expertise to our programs. The founder, a lecturer at Zambian Royal Medical University, ensures initiatives are research-informed, inclusive, and impactful. Our team is committed to transparency, collaboration, and continuous improvement to serve students effectively.

Community Engagement

LUWI Foundation empowers students to take active roles in their communities. Through service projects, awareness campaigns, talent exhibitions, and mentorship initiatives, we instill leadership, responsibility, and civic engagement. Our programs strengthen students individually while uplifting communities collectively.
