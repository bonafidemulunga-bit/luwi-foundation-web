About LUWI Foundation

Empowering students and communities in Zambia through education, awareness, and support programs.

Our Mission

LUWI Foundation is dedicated to creating awareness, providing educational support, and uplifting vulnerable students across Zambia. We focus on fostering an inclusive environment for learning, personal growth, and empowerment.

What We Do

Through workshops, school visits, and community programs, LUWI Foundation reaches hundreds of students every year. We partner with schools, universities, and organizations to deliver impactful sessions on education, health, and social awareness.

Our programs tackle real student struggles such as financial limitations, mental health challenges, and lack of access to critical resources. By engaging with pupils, college, and university students directly, we provide practical guidance and mentorship.
