About LUWI Foundation

Executive Summary

LUWI Foundation is a Zambia-based non-profit organization dedicated to raising awareness on intersex and sexual health issues among students and young people. Since its inception, the foundation has reached over 1,000 students across multiple schools and universities, organized numerous workshops and events, and formed strategic partnerships with organizations such as the Intersex Society of Zambia, Sports Impact Network, and Zambian Royal Medical University.

The foundation’s mission is to empower students with knowledge, provide safe spaces for dialogue, reduce stigma, and support young people in navigating sexual health challenges. Through sustained awareness programs, advocacy, and collaboration, LUWI Foundation seeks to create a lasting impact in Zambia’s educational and health sectors.

Mission

To educate, empower, and support students and young people in Zambia on intersex and sexual health issues, creating informed, confident, and stigma-free communities.

Vision

A Zambia where students and young people have access to comprehensive sexual health knowledge, support systems, and the confidence to make informed decisions about their well-being.

Our Achievements

  • Visited more than five schools and colleges to conduct awareness programs.
  • Reached over 1,000 students with workshops and training sessions.
  • Forged partnerships with the Intersex Society of Zambia, Sports Impact Network, and Zambian Royal Medical University.
  • Organized numerous community events and educational campaigns.
  • Collected and documented student testimonies to inform future programs.

Impact

Through our programs, students have gained critical knowledge on intersex and sexual health issues, enabling them to make informed choices. The foundation has provided safe spaces for discussions, encouraged peer-to-peer education, and reduced misinformation and stigma. Feedback from students highlights the transformative effect our workshops have on their confidence, health awareness, and ability to support peers.

Leadership

LUWI Foundation is led by a dedicated team of educators and advocates. The founder currently serves as a lecturer at Zambian Royal Medical University, combining academic expertise with grassroots awareness programs to bridge the gap between knowledge and practice.

Support Our Mission
